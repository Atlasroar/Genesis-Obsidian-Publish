**Royal Soldier Drones**can be found protecting [](The_Queen.md) in the village [](Western_Hive_(Location).md) and are part of the [](Western_Hive.md) faction.

Upon entering the Hill Hut located atop the village they will rush over
to you and demand you to leave. Any harm to the Queen will see you
facing all nearby Drones**.**

Despite their name, they have the same gear as the rest of their [](Soldier_Drone.md).
