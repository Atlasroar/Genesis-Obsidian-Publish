The **Small Thieves' Backpack** is a small
[backpack](Backpacks.md "wikilink") that reduces encumbrance by 60% and
does not hinder your stealth or combat abilities. It is a smaller
version of the [Thieves Backpack](Thieves_Backpack.md "wikilink").

Small Thieves Backpacks have an inventory size of **4x4**, making it
slightly larger than a [Small Backpack](Small_Backpack.md "wikilink"). When
equipped in the BACKPACK slot, this backpack adds a backpack inventory
with a size of **8x8**.

It can be purchased from General [Item Trader](Item_Trader.md "wikilink")
at [World's End](World's_End.md "wikilink") settlement, many backpack
stores such as the one in [Heft](Heft.md "wikilink") and
[Sho-Battai](Sho-Battai.md "wikilink") for c. 3,000, as well as [](Shinobi_Thieves.md) for c. 1,500.

## Trivia

- Small Thieves Backpack is the only Backpack which has no penalty for
  wearing.
- If carrying just an Advanced First Aid Kit or Skeleton Repair Kit
  (both at 2 kg) you will have less encumbrance wearing this backpack as
  it only weighs 1 kg and reduces the weight of items held inside by
  60%.
- If you wish to sneak but are wearing Heavy Armour you can fit your
  Armour, Pants and Shirt inside of the Small Thieves Backpack.
