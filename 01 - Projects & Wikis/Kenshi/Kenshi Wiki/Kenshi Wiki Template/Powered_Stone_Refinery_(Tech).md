**Powered Stone Refinery** is [Industry Tech](Industry_Tech.md "wikilink")
which allows players to construct [](Stone_Processor.md).

eventually [Hybrid Stone Mine](Hybrid_Stone_Mine_(Tech).md "wikilink"). The
