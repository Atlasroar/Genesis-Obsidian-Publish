**Gohan** (ご飯, "cooked rice" in Japanese) is a [Food](Food.md "wikilink")
item purchased from relevant [Traders](Traders.md "wikilink") or created in
a [Cooking Stove](Cooking_Stove.md "wikilink"). It has the same nutritional
value as a [Foodcube](Foodcube.md "wikilink"). This food is easiest to find
in [The Swamp](The_Swamp.md "wikilink"), where riceweed is abundant. It
will also be sold at a very cheap markup in places which use the
"swamper" Trade Culture, which causes it have the best nutritional/cat
value.

### Locations

- [The Dancing Skeleton](The_Dancing_Skeleton.md "wikilink")
