A **Rum Still** converts [Cacti](Cactus.md "wikilink") into [](Cactus_Rum.md).

Using the Rum Still trains the [Cooking](Cooking.md "wikilink") stat, and
is affected by the cooking time reduction multiplier that a higher
Cooking level brings.

NB This building does not require [](Empty_Rum_Bottle.md) to store the Rum.

## Details

- Power Consumption - -5
- Efficiency - 1x
- Workers - 1
