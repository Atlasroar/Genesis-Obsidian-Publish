**Empire Peasants** are characters that belong to the faction of the
[same name](02%20-%20Projects%20&%20Wikis/Kenshi/Kenshi%20Wiki/Kenshi%20Wiki%20Template/Empire_Peasants.md "wikilink"), and can be found in [](02%20-%20Projects%20&%20Wikis/Kenshi/Kenshi%20Wiki/Kenshi%20Wiki%20Template/United_Cities.md) territory. They either reside in UC
towns, or in small encampments in the desert. They are the underclass of
the Empire, a notable number of them being [Hivers](Hive.md "wikilink").

## Trivia

- An Empire Peasant in the [Old Village](Old_Village.md "wikilink") may
  approach the player and offer to join them. They will change their
  mind after being chastised by another peasant, however.
- Hiver Worker Drones and Hiver Soldier Drones make up the majority of
  the Empire Peasants, Worker Drones in particular are 4 times more
  likely to spawn as one when compared to your average
  [Greenlander](Greenlander.md "wikilink").
