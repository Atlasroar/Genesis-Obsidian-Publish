The **Storage: Armour Plate** is a [Storage](Storage.md "wikilink")
container which exclusively contains [](Armour_Plating.md). Players can construct this building
after researching [Storage Boxes: Crafting
(Tech)](Storage_Boxes:_Crafting_(Tech) "wikilink").

This container can hold a total of 25 plates.

Having Storage: Armour Plate is recommended for outposts with [](Armour_Smith.md).
