**Dried Meat** is a [Food](Food.md "wikilink") item purchased from relevant
[Traders](Traders.md "wikilink"), typically Bars. Often, players can find
dried meat through looting campfires found in the world.

Dried Meat is made from [Raw Meat](Raw_Meat.md "wikilink") at either a
[Cooking Stove](Cooking_Stove.md "wikilink") or a
[Campfire](Campfire.md "wikilink").
