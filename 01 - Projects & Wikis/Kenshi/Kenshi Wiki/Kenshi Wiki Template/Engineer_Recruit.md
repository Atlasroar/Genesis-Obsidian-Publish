**Engineer Recruit** is a randomly named [](Generic_Recruits.md). They're just an engineer looking
for work. They can be hired for 6,000 Cats.

Their race is determined by the weighted chances set in the
[Drifters](Drifters.md "wikilink") faction.

This character's entry in [FCS](Forgotten_Construction_Set.md "wikilink")
is named "hire engineer".
