Southern Hive
Princes are a Race in Kenshi. They are a subrace
of the Southern Hive.

Compared to their Western cousins, the
Southern Hive Prince have a lesser bleeding rate of 0.2 compared to the
0.3 of a Western Hive Prince, and also have
no bonuses of xp boost from Lockpicking,
Perception and
Toughness like they do. Southern Hive Princes
have slightly more health than their hungrier
Deadhive bretheren and
have xp bonuses in Field Medic and
Science.

Characters of this race can become if bought or freed from
slavery.
